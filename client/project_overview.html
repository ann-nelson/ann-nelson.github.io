    Project Overview

    Description

    The website, BrushedNBuilt, is a portfolio for an artist, designed to showcase their work and professional achievements. This portfolio will help the artist increase their visibility, attract potential clients, and serve as a central hub for their work.

    Purpose

    Potential clients looking for art commissions or other related skill sets, art enthusiasts, or individuals interested in the artist's unique work and style, as well as collaborators and fellow artists seeking partnership opportunities.

    Content

    Website will include:

    • Homepage: A brief introduction to the artist with featured work.
    • About Me: Background, artistic style, and career highlights.
    • Photo GalleryA curated gallery of the artist’s best works.
    • Client Feedback: Testimonials and reviews from previous clients.
    • Contact: Contact form which potential clients and collaborators can make inquiries.

    • Notes:


    For the artist’s website, I plan to implement several interactive JavaScript features to enhance user experience.

    • The Photo Gallery will feature a carousel, allowing users to view artwork in full-screen mode with smooth transitions between images. This will engage visitors and showcase the artist’s work in an interactive way. An example of this functionality can be seen on Unsplash.
    • On the Client Feedback page, I’ll include a testimonial carousel where clients can scroll through reviews, with both automatic and manual navigation options to keep the page dynamic and easy to navigate. Similar testimonial carousels can be found on Airbnb.
    • For the Contact Form, I’ll incorporate form validation and AJAX submission, allowing users to send inquiries without page reloads, enhancing convenience. Example of interactive forms include Google Forms.
    • The About Page will feature collapsible sections to organize the artist’s biography and achievements, making the page more user-friendly and clean, much like the layout on Wikipedia
    • Lastly, Scroll Animations will trigger as users scroll down the page, with text and images smoothly animating into view, making the website more dynamic. Scroll animations can be seen on sites like Spotify.
