Hi! My name is Amber Nelson and BrushedNBuilt is my portfolio that consists of work from drawn to built.


Brief Bio

My journey as an artist started in my home state Virgina but majority of my work has been done in North Carolina. My artwork consists of typical artwork such as paintings, drawings, and sculptures, but some of my artwork and skill is reflected in the construction work I have done such as tilework, bricklaying, and many other projects.
