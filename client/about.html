About the Artist

Name: Amber Nelson

Location: Raleigh, NC, USA

Mediums: Painting, Digital Art, Drafting, structures, etc.

Bio

Amber Nelson is an artist who adopts and experments with variety fo styles whose work explores themes of longlasting art woven into structures. With a background in self-taught experience, she brings a unique perspective to each piece, combining variety styles. Amber Nelson's work has been exhibited at the NC State Fair, homes and continues to evolve through experimentation and collaboration.



Artist Statement

"My work is a reflection to many artist and builders that has bridged the delciatcy of art and the strength of structures into everlasting artwork. I aim to combine artwork into homes and structures."
